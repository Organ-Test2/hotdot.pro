SITE FOR A COMPANY «SOYUZAVIA GROUP»

Mission

Site for a company carrying out delivery of aircraft equipment and providing aircraft-related repairs.

The key colours of the site's color scheme are:
Blue, colour of the skies. It symbolizes trust, professionalism and knowledge. Red, colour of the brave. It draws attention immediately, being full of energy and strength. Green, colour of the growth, durability and stability. It builds trust toward the company. These colours, put together with their variations, become powerful tool which draws visitors' attention and motivates them for ongoing use of the site.

Design concept was complemented with wonderful Fontatica font, which brilliantly conveys both dynamism of the company and stability of the whole industry.
