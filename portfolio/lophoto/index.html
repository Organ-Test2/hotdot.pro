Mary Lomova Logo and Website

Objective

Our task was to develop a unique logo and website for the photographer Mary Lomova.

We have combined the eye-filling typography with an element which would make play with the symbol and the name letters.

Draft logo variants

Minimalistic design and original navigation do not draw user’s attention away from the essential – the photographer’s works.

This website was developed with HTML5 with additional optimization for mobile devices.
